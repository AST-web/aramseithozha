Our Volunteer Opportunities

We have a variety of roles that cater to different skills and time commitments. Find what best suits you!

Education Support Volunteer

Assist in our tuition centers, help with lesson planning, or tutor students in various subjects.

  • Experience with children or teaching is a plus.
  • Minimum commitment: 4 hours/week.
  • Location: Chennai-based tuition centers.

Community Health Assistant

Help organize medical camps, assist medical professionals, or raise health awareness in villages.

  • Interest in public health or basic medical knowledge.
  • Flexible commitment based on camp schedules.
  • Location: Various project villages.

Fundraising & Outreach Coordinator

Support our fundraising initiatives, help manage social media, or engage with potential donors.

  • Strong communication and organizational skills.
  • Remote or in-office flexibility.
  • Minimum commitment: 5 hours/week.

Village Development Facilitator

Work directly with communities on empowerment projects, such as water management or sustainable agriculture.

  • Adaptability and cross-cultural communication skills.
  • Requires field visits and potentially longer commitments.
  • Location: Designated village project sites.

Administrative Support

Assist with general office tasks, data entry, report preparation, or managing volunteer records.

  • Good organizational and computer skills.
  • Flexible hours, mostly remote or office-based.
  • Minimum commitment: 3 hours/week.

Don't see a perfect fit? Tell us about your skills!
